Why Choose Gold Dust Croton?

The Gold Dust Croton (Codiaeum variegatum 'Gold Dust') is a stunning evergreen shrub, famous for its dark green leaves generously splashed with bright, sunny-yellow spots. It's more than a plant—it's a statement piece for any room.

☀️

Light

Thrives in bright, indirect light.

💧

Water

Keep soil consistently moist, but not soggy.

💦

Humidity

Loves a humid environment. A weekly misting is appreciated!

🌱

Benefit

A natural air purifier, known to remove toxins from your home.
